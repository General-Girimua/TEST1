About Mathletica

Discover our mission and what makes our mathematics community unique

Journey with Numbers

Mathletica is the Math Club founded for people who seek to embark on a journey with numbers: explore, question and deeply understand areas of mathematics.

Our club helps members learn advanced math topics, engage in research, and support one another in developing stronger conceptual foundations.

Mathletica also works to make mathematics accessible to everyone by providing free math education to underprivileged students in rural areas.
